Who we are

One Family Many Locations

We are confident that God is actively at work just like he was yesterday, and He continues to do so today. This is what DwellPraise is all about. It has evolved from the prayers of our founders Remoir and Angela into an international body that lives out and reflects His Word. Our dream today is to have thriving churches planted in the nations, where the Lord truly resides in our praises.

How It All
Started

history
2023

The Tales Seed

A relationship breakup drives Remoir to deepen his walk with God. Unable to find a formal place to serve, he gathers 10 friends into a WhatsApp group called "Tales" and commits to sending a story about a Bible chapter every day for a year — approximately 360 stories. A prophet, Bishop Isaiah Mbuga, Lead Pastor, Christ Heart Ministries, tells the youth: "You guys are the Next Generation." That word becomes a permanent anchor.

2024

Worship Harvest & Aethelwine

Entering university, Remoir meets Angela and aligns with Worship Harvest Nairobi and is placed under the mentorship of Papa Melo. After a three-week prayer period, he launches the Aethelwine Family Pseudo Missional Community with 5 people. He begins studying Bishop Dag Heward-Mills and Apostle Moses Mukisa on shepherding and financial growth — building the intellectual architecture for what would later become DwellPraise.

2025

The Hidden Season

Aethelwine grows to 50 members. During a 50-hour prayer marathon, at the 25th hour, the Holy Spirit reveals the YMG (You Must Grow) strategy for small-group management. But because the fellowship lacks an ecclesiastical covering, Remoir is instructed to disband it and send every member back to their churches. Deeply grieved but obedient, he transitions into the media team at WH Ongata Rongai. This is the "Nazareth" season — the seed falling into the ground.

January 23, 2026

Encounter Foundation

From a deep conviction about generosity, founders Remoir and Angela establish the Encounter Foundation — an initiative to be generous once a month through small, consistent habits. They open the Encounter Giving account and adopt a structured allocation architecture to industrialize their prosperity for the mission.

May 5, 2026

DwellPraise Launches

Led by the Holy Spirit, Remoir and Angela officially launch DwellPraise Ministries — WhatsApp channel, Substack, Instagram, YouTube, and social platforms go live. The transition from private incubation to public manifestation. On a bus days later, the Holy Spirit supernaturally convinces Remoir of the shepherdorial burden for 120 souls. The seed that fell into the ground in Nazareth is now rising in Galilee.

June 1, 2026

MakeMission Initiated

A for-profit initiative is launched to fund the Gospel and develop skills and talents for the mission. The economic engine behind the vision — "Once you start the process of building, the money will come." From manual labor to the industrialization of soul-winning.
